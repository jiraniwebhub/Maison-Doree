Our Story

Born from a Passion
For the Perfect Bite

Maison Dorée began as a dream shared over a worn kitchen table — a belief that food is the most honest form of love. Our founders, a duo of classically trained chefs who travelled from Paris to Marrakech sourcing flavour, returned home with one mission: to create a space where every guest feels the warmth of a private dinner.

We work with local farmers, seasonal produce, and age-old techniques reimagined for the modern palate. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just honest, beautiful food in a space designed to make you linger.
